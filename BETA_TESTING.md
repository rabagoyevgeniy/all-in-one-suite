# All-In-One Suite — Beta Testing Plan

## 1. Goal

- **Primary:** Identify and fix critical bugs before public launch.
- **Secondary:** Gather user feedback on usability, features, and overall experience.
- **Target:** 10-15 active beta testers representing all key roles (Parents, Coaches, Students).

---

## 2. Tester Recruitment (10-15 users)

| Role | Count | Recruitment Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Parent** | 5-7 | Existing loyal customers, personal contacts |
| **Coach** | 3-5 | Current coaching staff |
| **Student** | 2-3 | Children of recruited parents/coaches |

### Communication Channel

- Create a private **WhatsApp or Telegram group** for all beta testers.
- Use this for announcements, feedback collection, and support.

---

## 3. Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Send Welcome Email

- **Subject:** Welcome to the All-In-One Suite Beta!
- **Content:**
  - Thank them for participating.
  - Link to the live app on Vercel: `https://your-app.vercel.app`
  - Link to the WhatsApp/Telegram group.
  - Link to the feedback form (Google Forms).
  - Brief overview of what to test.

### Step 2: Provide Test Accounts

- **Parents:** Create accounts manually and send login details.
- **Coaches:** Use the Invite Code system to have them register.
- **Students:** Parents will create accounts for their children during onboarding.

### Step 3: Initial Guidance

- Post a welcome message in the group chat.
- Encourage them to complete the in-app onboarding flow.
- Provide a link to a short (2-3 min) Loom video showcasing key features.

---

## 5. Feedback Collection

### 5.1 Feedback Form (Google Forms)

Create a simple form with these fields:

- **Your Name:**
- **Your Role:** (Parent, Coach, Student)
- **Page/Feature:** (e.g., Booking Calendar, Payment Page)
- **Feedback Type:** (Bug Report, Feature Request, Usability Issue, General Comment)
- **Description:** (Please be as detailed as possible)
- **Screenshot/Video:** (Optional but helpful)

### 5.2 Bug Reporting

- **Critical Bugs:** (e.g., can't log in, payment fails) -> Report immediately in the group chat.
- **Non-Critical Bugs:** -> Use the Google Form.

### 5.3 Weekly Check-in

- Post a summary of fixed bugs and new features at the end of each week.
- Ask a general question to stimulate feedback (e.g., "What was the most confusing part of the app this week?").

---

## 6. Incentives

To encourage active participation, offer incentives:

- **Top Bug Finder:** 1 month free Premium subscription.
- **Most Valuable Feedback:** 5 free lesson credits.
- **All Participants:** 20% discount on their first subscription after launch.

---

## 7. Beta Completion Criteria

The beta test will conclude when:

- No new critical bugs have been reported for 7 consecutive days.
- At least 80% of the core testing scenarios have been successfully completed by testers.
- Key feedback on usability has been addressed.

**Estimated Duration:** 2-3 weeks.
